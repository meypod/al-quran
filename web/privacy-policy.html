Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 3, 2025

Al-Quran - Simple ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Al-Quran is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data. The app operates entirely offline and all data is stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All application data, including but not limited to:

  • Reading progress
  • User preferences and settings

is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Permissions

Al-Quran may request the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your preferences and reading progress locally on your device.

These permissions are used solely for the functionality of the app and no data leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Al-Quran does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use cookies, trackers, or any other technology to monitor your usage of the app.

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no privacy concerns regarding children's use of the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or transmit any information, the security of your data is primarily dependent on your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your device secure and up to date.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over all app data stored on your device and can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data through your device settings.
